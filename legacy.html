Our Journey

Today, ZEVAR is well-recognized as one of the most renowned and trusted jewellery brand not only in Porbandar district but much beyond it with wide customer base beyond India. It has been 101 years of journey, startingwith small-scale traditional business set up which has now transformed into 3-storeyed showroom that’s the biggest jewellery showroom in Porbandar district. In 1918, Soni Liladharbhai Nanjibhai started the jewellery business in an ancestral village near Porbandar. With a hard work ethic and customer focus approach,he was able to establish himself as reputable jewellerof the Barda region. When his son Soni Pratapbhai Liladharbhai joined his business, he recognized the growth potential and moved the business to Porbandar city in 1974.


Growth, Our vision

Pratapbhai broadened the customer base in Porbandar while retaining the long-established Barda region customer base. Their time-honored approach of offering traditional design while maintaining quality paved the way for the business to expand and provided a strong foundation to next generation to take it further. Sandip and Jayesh now represents 3rd generation of family and managing the business operations and growth for the company. With the vision of transforming traditional shop to modern showroom offering exquisite design concept, “ZEVAR” was launched in 2007 in Porbandar on Kirti Mandir road. This proved to be “game-changer” move for family business. Inspired by its grand success, team ZEVAR decided to go even bigger in 2019 and opened up ananother showroom in Kirti mandir Road with office space of 3500 sq. ft - biggest of its kind in Porbandar district. ZEVAR offers whole new collection from various parts of India, Italy, Dubai, Turkey and Singapore With main focus on high-quality products, beautiful designs and customer service, ZEVAR continues to expand its horizons.


Our Future, Our dreams

Sandip and Jayesh aspires to take the company to newer heights, capitalizing on expertise gained from 101 years of family business, blended with modern technology. Their vision is to bring unique design collections from domestic as well as international markets to doorsteps of Porbandar and to provide personalized service to customer’s delight with aim to make ZEVAR a household name throughout India and abroad.
